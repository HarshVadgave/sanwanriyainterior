About the studio

A family studio devoted to timeless interiors.

What began in 1994 in a single Jaipur workshop is today a full-service luxury design practice — still family-owned, still obsessed with craft.

Home / About
Our Story

Craft, carried through three decades.

Ramesh Suthar founded Sanwariya Interior in 1994 with a small team of carpenters and a conviction: that a home should be built the way a piece of fine furniture is — patiently, honestly, and one detail at a time.

Thirty years on, we've delivered more than 500 residential and commercial projects across India. The studio has grown, the tools have modernised, and photorealistic 3D has replaced the pencil sketch — but the workshop culture has not changed.

Meet the founder

Ramesh Suthar, Principal Designer.

Trained in traditional woodcraft and self-taught in modern architecture, Ramesh-ji still personally reviews every project drawing before it leaves the studio. His son Pawan Suthar leads the design and communications team, bringing the studio into its digital chapter.

"A home is not a project. It's a promise we're making to our clients — that ten years from now, they'll still love what we built together."
