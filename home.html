Our Process

Five deliberate steps, zero surprises.

01

Consultation

We visit your space, listen to how you live, and outline the possibilities in a written creative brief.

02

Planning & Budgeting

A transparent scope, timeline and BOQ so you know exactly what you're investing in — before you invest.

03

3D Design

Photorealistic renders, material boards and sample panels for every finish, colour and texture.

04
