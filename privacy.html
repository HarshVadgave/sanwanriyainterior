Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with us.

Home / Privacy

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry, we collect your name, phone number, email address, project type, budget range and the brief you share with us. We do not collect financial account details through our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your details only to respond to your enquiry, prepare a proposal, coordinate site visits and communicate about your project. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Retention

Enquiries that do not convert into projects are retained for 24 months and then permanently deleted. Client project files are retained for the duration of our aftercare programme plus five years.

4. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies for basic functionality (theme preference, session state). We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

Contact form submissions are processed through EmailJS solely to deliver your message to our studio inbox. Newsletter subscriptions are managed through our own systems.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by writing to us at studio@sanwariyainterior.com. We will respond within seven working days.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at +91 84462 13222 or studio@sanwariyainterior.com.
